## Authority

Chapter 2 (Constitution) is the **highest operational authority**. Chapter 3 (Glossary) is the **official vocabulary**. Chapter 4 (Design Principles) is the **design standard** — Volumes 2–10 must align with all three.

Chapter 1 (Introduction) orients readers; it does not override the Constitution or subsequent volumes.
